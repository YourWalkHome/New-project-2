# Fancy A Fungi Website Framework

First-pass static website framework for Fancy A Fungi, a premium gourmet mushroom producer in the Lockyer Valley, Queensland.

## Pages

- `index.html` - Home page with hero, trust points, featured product, and enquiry CTA.
- `about.html` - Farm story, production philosophy, and sustainability focus.
- `products.html` - Current active product only: White Oyster Mushrooms.
- `ask-the-mushroom-whisper.html` - Guided knowledge experience for cooking, storage, learning, farm context, and Benny handoffs.
- `contact.html` - Enquiry form, contact details, and service area.

## Future Growth

The structure can expand through new pages or sections such as:

- Additional product detail pages
- Knowledge content, recipes, and cooking guides
- Workshops, events, or farm experiences

The launch site intentionally stays focused on the current business reality.
