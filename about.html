About the farm

A premium local producer built with patience and care

Fancy A Fungi is led by Benny in the Lockyer Valley, Queensland, with a focus on fresh gourmet mushrooms, careful production habits, and direct relationships with the people who use the produce.

The story

Growing the right way, one consistent harvest at a time

Fancy A Fungi is being developed as a calm, reliable mushroom farm rather than a rushed catalogue of possibilities. The current work is practical: validate production consistency, keep contamination risk controlled, and build supply relationships that make sense for the farm and its customers.

The first active product is White Oyster Mushrooms. They are grown with a harvest-to-order mindset, so conversations about supply can stay grounded in what is actually available and fresh.

That approach shapes the whole business: premium local produce, clear communication, sustainable inputs, and growth that happens when the farm is ready for it.
